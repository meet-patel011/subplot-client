Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Subplot is a movie and TV series discovery platform. We do not stream, host, or download any movies or TV shows.

Information We Collect

The Subplot offers optional account-based features. Users may choose to create an account to access features such as watchlists, reviews, ratings, and social interactions. When an account is created, limited information such as a username or email address may be collected solely for account management and feature functionality. This information is not shared with third parties.

The app may store limited anonymous data such as:

  • Watchlist items
  • User voting choices
  • Mood-based recommendations
  • Guest or user session status

This data is stored locally on the user’s device and is used only to improve the app experience.

Third-Party Services

Movie and TV show information is provided by The Movie Database (TMDB).

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. An uses a News API key from GNews to fetch news articles related to movies and TV shows.

When users choose to watch content, they may be redirected to official third-party platforms. The Subplot is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.
